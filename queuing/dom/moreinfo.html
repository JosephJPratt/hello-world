About

An all-age dementia information resource. We aim to help people with dementia, their carers and health professionals find information about dementia and the support and care services available to them. For information on the range of dementia services available please see the Information Prescriptions for your local area.

Alzheimer's Society is a membership organisation, which works to improve the quality of life of people affected by dementia in England, Wales and Northern Ireland.
